Personal Artifacts

Rift - An Interactive Comic

This artifact is an interactive comic built in Unity based on the quantum theory of superposition. We were tasked to create a narrative on the topic with multiple interactive elements within the comic, along with animations totalling to 30 seconds. I learned a lot more about unity canvas and interface, as well as a lot more art techniques.

Pizza Hut Website Redesign Animation

Tasked to redesign any website implementing learned UI/UX Techniques. We focused on prototyping and user research. I learned a lot more about prototypes and how users can change the final product. I loved seeing the changes I made and how they truly made the website better after I changed some elements.

Legend of Zelda Video Edit Tribute

A ~1 minute video to a song, with Legend of Zelda gameplay overlaid with video transitions. I learned a lot more stuff on Adobe After Effects. It was very fun for me because I love both the song and the media I used.
